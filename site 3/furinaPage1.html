Curiosities


Furina is not considered an Archon, as she did not carry any of the divinity of Focalors and was fully human. This is further indicated by the conversation between Focalors and Furina shown in The Opera of Noirceur and Blancheur, in which Focalors tasks Furina with pretending to be a god without being one or attempting to become one. Despite the urgency and great importance of Furina's task, Focalors did not provide Furina with anything that would help make Furina's false identity
